Sprint 3 Reflection

I think the team did well to improve upon the shortcomings of sprint 2. The work was spaced out and planned ahead in a far more organized manner which allowed us to finish core functionality far more quickly compared to last sprint. Furthermore, communication has improved quite a lot which has also been a help in boosting team productivity. Nevertheless, there are always things to improve upon for future sprints. Foremost I think we as a team could do a better job at emphasizing refactoring existing portions of code. Certain classes and methods (i.e. the keyboard controller or LoadContent() method) are a bit messy and could be greatly improved with some refactoring. Further, leaving some additional time for code reviews and discussion could be helpful. In general, I think the team made great strides in sprint 3 with lots of opportunities for growth in future sprints.
